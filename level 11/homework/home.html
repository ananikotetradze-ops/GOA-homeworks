chicken shawarma

description:

Chicken shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made with marinated and grilled chicken, typically served in a pita bread with various toppings and sauces. The chicken is usually seasoned with a blend of spices such as cumin, paprika, garlic, and turmeric, giving it a flavorful and aromatic taste. It is often accompanied by fresh vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions, along with sauces like tahini or garlic sauce. Chicken shawarma is a delicious and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed as a quick street food or as part of a larger meal.

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into strips
  • tomatoes
  • cucumbers
  • onions
  • mayonnaise and ketchup
  • lettuce
  • pita bread

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken strips with your choice of marinade (such as a mixture of yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, and spices). Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken until it is cooked through and has a nice char on the outside, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, prepare your toppings by slicing the tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions.
  4. Once the chicken is cooked, let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into smaller pieces.
  5. Warm the pita bread and then fill it with the cooked chicken and your desired toppings. Drizzle with mayonnaise and ketchup or your preferred sauces.
  6. Serve the chicken shawarma immediately and enjoy!
