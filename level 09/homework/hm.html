Bunnies

Bunnies are small, furry animals that are known for their long ears and fluffy tails. They are often kept as pets and are also found in the wild.

Bunnies are herbivores, which means they eat plants. They enjoy eating a variety of vegetables, fruits, and hay. They are also known for their ability to reproduce quickly, which is why they are often associated with fertility.

In addition to being cute and cuddly, bunnies are also very social animals. They enjoy the company of other bunnies and can form strong bonds with their human caretakers. They are also known for their playful nature and can be quite entertaining to watch.
